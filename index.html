Education Overview

2016-2020: Martin L. Nesbitt Discovery Academy/Asheville Buncombe Technical Community College

My interest in technology started early, with my application and enrollment to Nesbitt Discovery Academy, the only STEM focused high school in Buncombe County, North Carolina. During my time here I was able to pursue various dual enrollment opportunities through AB-Tech, a local community college. This experience not only prepared me for full-time college enrollment, but greatly expedited the process of completing my undergraduate studies.

2020-2024: Rochester Institute of Technology

In 2020 I enrolled in the five-year computer science program at Rochester Institute of Technology, which includes four years of classes, two semester-long internships, and one summer internship. Thanks to the transfer credits that I received from my dual enrollment classes in high school and a couple of summer classes, I was able to graduate a year early in 2024 with my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science.
